Deena Tenzer; Workshop Leader
Deena Tenzer is a passionate Software Engineer currently working at Snapsheet. Born and raised in Chicago, she received a bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Mathematics from the University of Wisconsin-Madison. Deena returned to Chicago after school and has been working in the technology industry for over three years. Deena dedicates her time to promoting women in STEM by facilitating the Girls Who Code at Walter Payton, as well as sitting on the Illinois Technology Association's Women Influence Chicago Jr. board.
